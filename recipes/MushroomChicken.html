Creamy Chicken and Mushrooms

Description

Whether you scored wild mushrooms at the farmers' market or just have some baby bellas on hand, this healthy creamy chicken recipe is delicious with any of them. Serve over whole-wheat egg noodles or mashed potatoes.

Ingredients (Serves 4)
  • 4 to 5-ounce Chicken Cutlets
  • 4 Cups Mixed Mushrooms, sliced if large
  • ½ Cup Dry White Wine.
  • ½ Cup Heavy Cream
  • 2 Tablespoons Finely Chopped Fresh Parsley

Directions
  1. Sprinkle chicken with 1/4 teaspoon each kosher salt and pepper.
  2. 1 tablespoon canola oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Cook the chicken, turning once, until browned and just cooked through, 7 to 10 minutes total. Transfer to a plate.
  4. Add 1 tablespoon oil and mushrooms to the pan; cook, stirring occasionally, until the liquid has evaporated, about 4 minutes.
  5. Increase heat to high, add wine and cook until it has mostly evaporated, about 4 minutes.
  6. Reduce heat to medium; stir in cream, any accumulated juice from the chicken and 1/4 teaspoon each salt and pepper.
  7. Return the chicken to the pan and turn to coat with the sauce. Serve the chicken topped with the sauce and sprinkled with parsley.

Nutrition Facts (Per Serving)
  • 325 Calories
  • 20g Fat
  • 4g Carbs
  • 29g Protein
